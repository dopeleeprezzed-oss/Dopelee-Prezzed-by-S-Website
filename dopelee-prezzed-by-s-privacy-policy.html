Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Dopelee Prezzed collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services and social media integrations.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information when you connect your social media accounts
  • Social Media Data: Public profile information, page access tokens, and content publishing permissions you authorize
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services
  • Content: Images, text, and other content you upload for social media publishing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Schedule and manage your social media posts
  • Provide analytics and insights about your content performance
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services

3. Social Media Permissions

When you connect social media accounts, we request only the permissions necessary to:

  • Post content to your pages and profiles
  • Read basic account information
  • Access engagement metrics and insights

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your social media account settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure

5. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following platforms:

  • Facebook & Instagram (Meta Platforms)
  • TikTok
  • LinkedIn
  • Pinterest
  • YouTube (Google)

Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs the use of your data on their services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Opt out of communications
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you disconnect your accounts or request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
